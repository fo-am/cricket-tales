ABOUT THE PROJECT

In a secret field in Northern Spain, 300 cricket burrows are monitored using CCTV. This is the first long-term study of behaviour in a wild insect, and we need your help to see what the crickets are up to.

By tagging events in these videos, you contribute directly to research, enabling a better understanding of the crickets' personalities and how their lives progress. This helps us to find out more about animal behaviour and the ageing process.
